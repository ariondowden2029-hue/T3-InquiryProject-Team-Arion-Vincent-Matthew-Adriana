This is Our About us and Contact Page

La Snackeria NYC is a women-owned, Latina-owned restaurant in Sunset Park, Brooklyn, that specializes in Mexican street food with Ecuadorian influences. Famous for its viral, savory birria tacos and aesthetic pink decor, the cozy shop offers a fun, welcoming space complete with board games.

Location and Hours

Adress: 5823 5th Avenue, Brooklyn, NY 11220

Hours: Open daily from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM (Closed on Mondays)

Ordering: Dine-in, takeout, and local delivery are all available via platforms like Grubhub and Seamless
